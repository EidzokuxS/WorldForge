# 82-05 Plan - Integrated Live Play Gate

## Goal

Prove the dynamic staging kit works in a real campaign and improves playability rather than merely satisfying schemas.

## Requirements Covered

P82-R1 through P82-R8.

## Tasks

1. Run focused backend and frontend suites covering Phase 82 seams.
2. Start clean backend/frontend dev servers.
3. Generate a fresh random campaign with a non-reused premise.
4. Use Playwright and/or API/SSE logs to play opening plus at least 10 turns.
5. Force one natural absent-local-affordance case and one support-NPC case.
6. Ask for a similar affordance again and prove reuse-before-create instead of duplicate minting.
7. Verify cleanup/promotion behavior after moving away or ending the scene.
8. Inspect SQLite/LanceDB/currentScene/narration for false or leaked settled facts.
9. Write `82-VERIFICATION.md` and `82-SUMMARY.md` with exact campaign id, turn stages, tool calls, screenshots/logs, residual risks, and requirement traceability.

## Verification

- Backend typecheck passes.
- Frontend typecheck passes.
- Focused deterministic suites pass.
- Live gate reaches opening plus at least 10 turns with dynamic creation, reuse-before-create, quantified no-spam, and cleanup/promotion proof.
- In 10 turns with at most two scene changes, at most two dynamic creations occur; ordinary follow-up turns with no new scene fiction create zero dynamic objects.
- GitNexus detect_changes reports expected scope or documented risk.

## Completion Notes

The live gate is blocking. If the game still cannot be played through this behavior, do not mark the phase complete.
