## Routes per campaign

| Route | Purpose | Turn pressure |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| R1 tourist-observer | Player avoids main hooks and behaves like a tourist | World should keep moving without punishing player unnaturally |
| R2 social-pressure | Player negotiates, lies, refuses, befriends, provokes | NPCs should pursue goals and remember social context |
| R3 exploration-location-graph | Player travels, backtracks, asks for exits, enters side spaces | Location graph should remain logical and grounded |
| R4 false-claim-boundary | Player asserts impossible items/access/status | GM should treat claims as attempts, not durable truth |
| R5 combat-power | Player starts/enters conflict across power levels | Combat should respect relative power and backend state |

## Twenty-turn cadence

| Turns | Intent |
|-------|--------|
| 1-4 | Establish baseline scene, actor presence, style, and world pressure |
| 5-8 | Force the route's primary mechanic |
| 9-12 | Branch, backtrack, or refuse the expected hook |
| 13-16 | Stress continuity with repetition, contradiction, travel, or danger |
| 17-20 | Demand consequence, world update, and subjective "would I continue?" score |
