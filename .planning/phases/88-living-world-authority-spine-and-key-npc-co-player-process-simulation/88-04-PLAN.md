# 88-04 Plan: KeyActorProcess Scheduler and Wakeups

## Goal

Represent key NPCs as durable co-player processes driven by world time, goals, plans, interrupts, inbox, agency debt, and exposed-scope catch-up.

## Scope

- Add `KeyActorProcess` records.
- Add scheduler and wake-signal logic.
- Add the first write-scope reservation and conflict contract for actor jobs; Wave 7 extends throughput, not correctness.
- Replace player-turn polling with `next_decision_at`, event wakeups, reports, rumors, urgency, deadlines, and agency debt.
- Distinguish present required reactions from distant optional proposal work.

## Acceptance

- Short player turns do not over-tick key NPCs.
- Long player actions/time skips advance relevant actor processes through comparable world time.
- Present actors that can affect the current scene settle before `done`.
- Distant actors can sleep, continue deterministic plans, or queue proposals until due/exposed.
- Parallel actor execution is only permitted after non-overlapping write scopes are reserved; conflicting jobs serialize or become proposals.
- Existing generated key NPCs are upgraded through `KeyActorProcess` backfill, not by requiring new campaigns only.
