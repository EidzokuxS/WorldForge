# 88-09 Plan: World Threads and Diegetic Surfacing

## Goal

Model long-running world pressure as durable WorldThreads with clocks, stages, involved actors/factions, source events, due points, surface routes, consequences, rumors, and digests.

## Scope

- Add world-thread records and runner.
- Keep forecasts advisory; world threads and actor/faction tools own committed state.
- Attach WorldThreads to the generic due-world-work/catch-up interface introduced in `88-06`.
- Add surface-route logic for sensory signs, witnesses, reports, rumors, changed locations, and later testimony.
- Integrate thread signals into PlayerFacingPacket without leaking hidden cause.

## Acceptance

- Tourist play advances world threads without forcing protagonist drama.
- Player-facing narration can show only committed visible signals until a valid knowledge route exists.
- Later investigation can reveal committed causes through state, witnesses, reports, or memory.
- Forecast-only pressure never appears as committed aftermath, thread stage, or narrator fact without a source event.
