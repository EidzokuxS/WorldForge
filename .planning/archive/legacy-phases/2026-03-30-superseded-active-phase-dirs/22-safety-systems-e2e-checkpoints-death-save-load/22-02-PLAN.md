3. **Checkpoint persistence across reload** (no LLM):

   - Reload the page (navigate to same URL)

   - Wait for page reload (loadCheckpoint triggers window.location.reload)
   - After reload, verify chat history matches the pre-turn state (checkpoint was created before the turn)
   - Screenshot: 22-02-task1-04-after-load.png

5. **Delete checkpoint via UI** (no LLM):
   - Open checkpoint panel
   - Click Delete on a checkpoint
   - Confirm the AlertDialog ("Delete checkpoint?")
   - Verify the checkpoint disappears from the list
   - Screenshot: 22-02-task1-05-after-delete.png
