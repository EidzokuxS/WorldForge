3. **Generate scaffold** via `POST http://localhost:3001/api/worldgen/generate` with body containing the campaign premise, seeds (from Task 1 suggest-seeds), and settings. This endpoint streams SSE events. Capture the full stream and verify:

   - Other sections (factions, npcs) are NOT affected
