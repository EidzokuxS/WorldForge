The Confrontation

Blood drips from the ceiling, hissing as it hits the magically superheated obsidian floor. Elaria clutches her side, leaning against a broken pillar, breathing heavily.

From the shadows of the throne, the Vampire Lord steps forward. His eyes burn like twin dying suns. He doesn't draw a weapon; he simply raises a pale hand, and the blood on the floor begins to levitate, forming sharp crimson needles pointed directly at you.
